No Data Storage

We do not store or retain any information you enter into CarbonClarity. All calculations are performed directly in your browser.

Local Calculations

Your data never leaves your device. The tool uses your inputs to calculate your carbon footprint locally—nothing is sent to our servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including your name, email, IP address, or any details entered in the calculator.

Security

CarbonClarity is designed to be secure. We use modern web standards to protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized access to your information.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party trackers, analytics, or advertising scripts. No cookies are set unless strictly necessary for basic site functionality.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or regulations. We will notify users of significant changes via our website.

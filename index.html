Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is CarbonClarity?
We use 2025-updated emission factors from IPCC, IEA, EPA, DEFRA, and global government sources. More accurate than 99% of calculators online.
Why include meat consumption?
Animal agriculture causes ~14.5% of global emissions. One vegetarian person saves ~1 ton CO₂e every year!
Can I become carbon neutral?
Yes! After calculating, we show exactly how many trees to plant or verified carbon credits to buy to offset 100%.
Is my data safe?
100% private. Nothing is stored. All calculations happen in your browser only. We don't collect or sell any data.
How much can one person really change?
One person reducing 5 tons = planting 230+ trees = removing 15 petrol cars for a year. Never underestimate your power!
What is the biggest source of carbon emissions?
For most people, electricity, transportation (especially flying), and food (especially meat and dairy) are the top three contributors.
How often should I calculate my footprint?
We recommend calculating quarterly or after major lifestyle changes to track your progress and stay motivated.

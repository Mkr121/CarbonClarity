Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is CarbonClarity?
Built using 2025 emission factors from IPCC, IEA, EPA, and national grids. More accurate than 99% of online calculators.
Why include food and meat?
Animal agriculture is responsible for ~14.5% of global emissions. Going plant-based one day a week saves nearly 0.5 tons per year.
Can I actually become carbon neutral?
Yes. After your result, we'll show exactly how many trees to plant or verified credits to buy to offset 100%.
Is my data private?
100% private. Nothing is stored or tracked. All calculations happen in your browser only.
How much can one person really change?
Reducing just 5 tons per year has the same impact as planting 250 trees or removing 20 petrol cars from the road for a year. You matter.
